How can I reach the customer support staff?

To reach the customer support staff, navigate to the contact page from the navigation bar on this page. Leave a message with the contact form and a chat ticket will be sent to your email. Our response is usually within 24 hours.

How can I become a Driver?

It's very easy to be a driver on CoRider. Just sign up and submit the necessary information about your vehicle for you to get verified. You can then start a ride as a driver.

How do you calculate the cost for the ride?

The cost of a ride per km is fixed. This has been calculated considering the current gas prices and a fair incentive for the drivers. You can share the cost of a ride with your CoRider. All payments are made directly to drivers and there are no hidden charges for us. You can view our Pricing page for more information.
